Research Overview

Research Interests

Science of Science Communication

My research integrates scholarly publication data with information on public discussions about scientific topics across media platforms to analyze the process from scientific knowledge production to public discourse.

  • Scientists' use of social media
  • Online media coverage of scientific publications
  • Linguistic features in research papers, including hype and hedging expressions
[ Current Research Topic ]

Misinformation in and about Science

I investigate the spread of scientific misinformation, particularly misinformation originating within the research community itself. My research empirically examines how practices such as the dissemination of preprints and the promotion of scientific findings by researchers may unintentionally contribute to misinformation and its societal impact.
